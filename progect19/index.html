real estate
KAMA Incorporated provides investment consulting services in the field of overseas profitable real estate. The company provides customized solutions for individuals and legal entities who want to invest in overseas property.
The value of our services is that KAMA Incorporated accompanies you from the moment you choose an investment strategy until the moment the property is successfully resold (if necessary). We are responsible for the result of our work.
How do we work
how do we work

  • Determine your goals, budget and the final expectations from the investment

  • Choose a proper investment strategy

  • Offer the destinations (countries) under the chosen investment strategy

  • Form a report about taxes, buying process, expenses on a property management and resale

  • Send the investment objects to choose from

  • Defend the best terms of the deal

  • Perform an independent law audit of an investment object

  • Support of a property deal

  • In contact with you during all of the ownership period

  • Assist in a property resale (if needed)
